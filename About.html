About us

What is VIAFit?

VIAFit is fitness centre founded and designed in 1990. Since then, we helped thousands of members with their training, recovery and keeping their bodies healthy. Here, we ensure that you will find everything necessary to fulfill your needs, whether you want to build mass, improve stamina or any goal you set for yourself.

What can you find here?

At VIAFit you can find everything for youself, whether you are fitness enthusiast or you just started your adventure with the concept of fitness. Have you been thinking about powerlifting or experimenting with weights? We have fully functional gym with equipment for members of every strength. Do you want to test your stamina's limit? Feel free to use our section with adjusted treadmills and stationary bicycles. Or maybe you are interested in organised courses? Don't hesitate to choose any of our seven classes with certified instructors.

Our story

The beginning

It all started in 1989 when Rob Sixpack decided to pursue his dream and invested what he inherited from his uncle, Tom Biceps, into building a small fitness center that we all know and love now, more than 30 years later.

The candle incident

Only one year after the opening of our fitness center the bad luck struck us and half the building burned down because of a candle forgotten lit in the old archive. But one good thing came out of this. Rob Sixpack bought new bikes for the spinning class with the leftover money from the insurance and this is how Bob Sixpack, his son and now the owner of VIAFit, started training for what later became a spinning instructor superstar.
